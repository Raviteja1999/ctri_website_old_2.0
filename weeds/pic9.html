Tobacco Agridaksh
KNOWLEDGE BASED SYSTEM FOR TOBACCO WEEDS
ICAR-CENTRAL TOBACCO RESEARCH INSTITUTE,RAJAHMUNDRY


Scientific name:Dactyloctenium agyptium, Beauv.
Family:GRAMINEAE

Weed description
A short annual with creeping or erect branches. Stems spreading, smooth, rooting at lower nodes. Leaf blade linear, glaucous, 1- 6 inches long ; ligule short and membranous. Spikes digitate, 2- 6, 1- ½ inches long. Spikelets arranged in two rows on one side of the axis, 3 – 5 flowered, glumes 5 – 7. First glume ovate and second glume ovate, slightly awned. The rest of the glumes are flowering glumes enclosing hermaphrodite flowers.

CONTROL

  • Post emergence application of Quizalofop ethyl@ 60 g ai/ha is recommended during crop growth
  • Intercultural operations reduce the incidence of the weed in tobacco in the initial stages
  • Hand weeding regularly reduces the weed infestation
